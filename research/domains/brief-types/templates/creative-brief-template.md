**Contains Claims**: Rarely
**Claim Types**: N/A

### Section 4: Key Message / Value Proposition
**Contains Claims**: Always
**Claim Types**: Core product/service claims
**Extraction Priority**: Critical

### Section 5: Proof Points / RTBs (Reasons to Believe)
**Contains Claims**: Always
**Claim Types**: All types - substantiation claims
**Extraction Priority**: Critical

### Section 6: Mandatory Inclusions
**Contains Claims**: Often
**Claim Types**: Required legal claims
**Extraction Priority**: High

### Section 7: Competitive Context
**Contains Claims**: Sometimes
**Claim Types**: Comparative claims
**Extraction Priority**: High

### Section 8: Tone / Brand Guidelines
**Contains Claims**: Rarely
**Claim Types**: N/A
