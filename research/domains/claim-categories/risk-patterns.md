## Overview
Patterns and keywords that indicate high-risk claims requiring verification.

## Risk Level Definitions

| Level | Description | Action Required |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| Critical | Likely FTC violation | Block until verified |
| High | Requires substantiation | Flag for legal review |
| Medium | Should be verified | Recommend verification |
| Low | Standard claims | Normal processing |
| Minimal | Puffery/opinion | No action needed |
